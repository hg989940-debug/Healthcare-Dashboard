# 🏥 Healthcare Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing healthcare data to uncover key insights related to patient trends, hospital performance, and financial efficiency. The goal is to support data-driven decision-making in a healthcare environment.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze patient demographics and medical conditions
* Track hospital revenue and patient volume trends
* Evaluate doctor performance and operational efficiency
* Identify high-cost treatments and resource utilization
* Understand insurance provider contributions

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* **SQL** → Data Cleaning & Preprocessing
* **Power BI** → Data Visualization & Dashboard Development

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 🔹 Executive Overview

* Total Patients, Revenue, and Doctors
* Revenue vs Patient Volume analysis
* Admission trends over time
* Top-performing doctors

### 🔹 Patient & Medical Insights

* Medical condition analysis (frequency vs cost)
* Age group and gender distribution
* Medication and treatment insights
* Average hospital stay analysis

### 🔹 Financial & Performance Insights

* Insurance provider performance
* Doctor efficiency analysis
* Hospital performance comparison
* Revenue distribution and cost analysis

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Identified top revenue-generating hospitals and doctors
* Found high-demand medical conditions with high treatment costs
* Analyzed patient demographics for better service targeting
* Detected inefficiencies through hospital stay duration
* Evaluated insurance providers based on coverage and revenue contribution
